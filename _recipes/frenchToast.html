3 eggs

Beat together, and pour into a pie plate

25 ml sugar

5 ml salt

100 ml milk

Six slices of bread

Dip both sides into the egg mixture, then stack them in the egg mixture

Lightly oil a non-stick frying pan and heat it to medium heat. Lightly fry the bread until golden, flip and fry the second side. Serve with maple syrop and/or icing sugar
