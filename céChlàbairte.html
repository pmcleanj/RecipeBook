Cé Chlàbairte

(clotted cream)


500 ml heavy cream

Pour into a large crock-pot. Heat on Low for 12 hours. Skim thickened cream off and serve with scones. Reserve thin milk liquids for other baking.

Drop by spoonfulls onto cookie sheet and bake 10-12 minute
