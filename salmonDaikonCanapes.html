>Salmon Daikon Canapes

  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Combine cheese and juice in a bowl.

  • 4 ounces goat cheese, at room temperature

  • 12 ounces daikon, cut crosswise into 16 slices (1/4 inch thick each)

Spread a layer of cheese mixture on each daikon slice

8 ounces thinly sliced lox cut into 16 pieces

top with salmon and garnish with chive
