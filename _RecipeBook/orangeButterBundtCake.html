325 g flour

Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.

450 g sugar

10 ml baking powder

2 ml salt


225 g butter

Add butter and mix until blended.

4 eggs

3 eggyolks

mix eggs, egg yolk, milk, orange zest, and extracts

10 ml orange extract


125ml milk


Preheat oven to 325°F. Mix egg-mixture in thirds to flour-butter mixture. Fill a bundt pan and bake about one hour.
