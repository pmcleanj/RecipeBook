One cup dried beans (white, black or red – experiment!)

Soak the beans overnight in water, then drain, discarding the water

Four cups water

Two cups water

Pour over the beans in a stovetop pan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer covered until tender, about 90 minutes.
