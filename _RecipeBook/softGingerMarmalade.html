Cooked Citrus pieces from making Ginger Tea

Blend on the lowest setting just until slightly smooth. Put in a heavy-bottomed saucepan

1 cup white sugar

Stir into blended fruit

Simmer on very low heat until thickened. Store in sterilized 250ml jars.
