Mulled Cider

a quart of cider

Bring the cider and cloves to a boil.

10 cloves

1 cup of maple syrup

Add the syrup and stir until thoroughly mixed.

4 cinnamon sticks

Place a cinnamon stick in each mug and pour the liquid over it

nutmeg

Top with nutmeg.
