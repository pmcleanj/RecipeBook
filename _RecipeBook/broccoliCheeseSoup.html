--- bg: "denizens.jpg" layout: RecipeBook title: Broccoli and Cheese Soup crawlertitle: Logrus -Broccoli and Cheese Soup tags: Vegetarian Soup --- Stalks only of two broccoli crowns

Cut into thin rounds across the grain and simmer in unsalted water for an hour or more until soft. Drain, retaining the liquid.

150 grams low-fat chedder

Grate or chop fine in blender.

Blend soft broccoli and cheese together until smoothe. Add only as much of the retained liquid as required to reach the desired consistency. Return to the pot and heat gently.
